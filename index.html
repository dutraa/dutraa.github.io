About BSKTx

Basket Stablecoin (BSKTx) is backed by a diversified basket of real-world assets — 40% copper, 30% sugar, 20% natural gas, and 10% U.S. Treasuries. Through Chainlink Price Feeds, Proof of Reserve, and automated smart contract rebalancing, BSKTx maintains a $1 peg while reducing risk from volatility in any single asset.

Now live on Ethereum Sepolia and Base Sepolia testnets, enabling low-cost, high-speed governance and transactions.
